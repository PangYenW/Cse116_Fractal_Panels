In order to update the escape distance,
click on the JMenu "Choose Your Input", and then JMenuItem "Enter Escape Distance"
Enter an valid value to get the desired escape distance,
Or enter an invalid input to get an Exception.

In order to update the escape time,
click on  the JMenu "Choose Your Input" and then JMenuItem "Enter Escape Time"
Enter an valid input, 0< time <= 255
Or get an error message.
